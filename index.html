📝 Step-by-Step

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to submit your scholarship application

1
🔍

Research & Discover

Browse our scholarship listings and use filters to find opportunities that match your profile, field of study, and eligibility.

2
✅

Check Eligibility

Read the eligibility criteria carefully. Ensure you meet the academic, financial, and other requirements before applying.

3
📄

Gather Documents

Collect all required documents such as mark sheets, income certificates, ID proofs, and recommendation letters.

4
🖊️

Fill & Submit

Complete the application form on the official portal. Double-check all entries and upload the necessary documents.

5
📬

Track & Follow Up

Save your application reference number. Monitor your email and the scholarship portal for updates on your application status.
