      Document Center

      Essential documents, preparation tips, and downloadable templates.

      🪪

      ID Proof

      Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, or Voter ID. Keep scanned copies (PDF & JPEG) under 500KB.

      💰

      Income Certificate

      Issued by Tahsildar or SDM office. Valid for 1 year from issue date.

      📝

      Academic Records

      Mark sheets of last 2 years, bonafide certificate, migration certificate if applicable.

      🏦

      Bank Details

      Passbook front page or cancelled cheque. Ensure Aadhaar-linked for DBT.

      📷

      Passport Photo

      Recent photo, white background, 3.5 × 4.5 cm, under 200KB.

      📜

      Caste Certificate

      Required for reservation-based scholarships. Issued by SDM/Collector.

        💡 Application Tips

        01
        Start Early

        Gather documents 2–3 months before deadlines.

        02
        Digital Backups

        Scan everything in PDF & JPEG. Use cloud storage.

        03
        Cross-Check Details

        Names, dates & numbers must match across all documents.

        04
        Write Your SOP

        Draft early, get feedback, and revise at least twice.

        05
        Track Everything

        Use the dashboard to track deadlines and statuses.

        🧭 Step-by-Step Application Guide

        1
        Research

        Browse scholarships, check eligibility, save promising ones.

        2
        Prepare Documents

        Gather all required documents listed in the scholarship details.

        3
        Fill Application

        Complete the online form carefully, double-check every field.

        4
        Submit & Track

        Submit before deadline. Use the tracker to monitor your status.

        5
        Follow Up

        Check the portal regularly for verification requests or updates.
